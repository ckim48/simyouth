Our Mission

Empowering the Next Generation of Ethical Thinkers

SimYouth was born from a critical observation: while the rules of war—International Humanitarian Law—are taught in classrooms, the moral weight of applying them in the field is rarely felt by those who will inherit the future.

Our motivation is to bridge the gap between abstract legal principles and human empathy. By simulating the impossible choices faced by civilians, students, and medics, we allow users to develop a "moral compass" that functions under pressure.

"The goal is not just to teach what is legal, but to understand what is human when every option feels like a loss."

The SimYouth Journey

Step 01

Assume Your Role

Step into the life of a school student, a field medic, or a commander. Experience how different perspectives change the weight of every choice and reveal unique layers of the conflict.

Step 02

Navigate Dilemmas

Engage with scenarios built on IHL principles. Decide under pressure while the system tracks your alignment with International Humanitarian Law principles like Distinction and Proportionality.

Step 03

Analyze & Reflect

Receive a comprehensive report and an AI-generated Reflective Journal. Understand your ethical framework and discover how your decisions compare to established legal standards.
